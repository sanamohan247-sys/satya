About Us

Learn more about our craftsmanship, dedication to farmer success, and the history behind Lakshmi Srinu Tractor Works.

Forging Field Solutions Since 2011

At Lakshmi Srinu Tractor Works, we recognize that standard farming machinery doesn't always handle local agricultural terrains. That's why we specialize in customizing standard machinery to adapt to rocky soil conditions, high-volume payloads, and wet paddy fields.

Founded by Srinu, our workshop in the agricultural hub has evolved from a small repair shop to a full-fledged tractor engineering and custom trolley fabrication facility. Every weld, axle, and hydraulic system is thoroughly tested to guarantee reliability when it matters most.
